Trevor Noah

Man of all nations

Biography

He was born on February 20,1984,in Johannesburg South Africa to a Xhosa mother and a swiss -German father
.Trevor moved to the united states and became the first south african comedian to appear on The Tonight Show with Jay Leno
.In 2014, he joined The Daily Show as a correspondant and he succeeded Jon Stewart as a host in 2015.

Achievements

  • His memoir ,Born a crime became a bestseller and received a widespread acclaim.
  • He won various awards including the Emmy awards.
  • He was named one of the 35 most powerful people in New York Media.
  • In 2018,he was named one of the hundred most influential people in the world .
