Introduction

Garissa county is situated in northeastern region of kenya whereby it shares its eastern borders with Somalia
. The county's capital and largest urban centre is Garissa town which is located along tana river.

Education

The county has numerous primary and secondary schools as well as technical institutions
.It is a home to Garissa University College which offers various academic programs .

Economy

The county is a market centre for the neighbouring regions.The town hosts various banks like Postbank
.Livestock rearing is a significant economic activity with cattle,camels and sheep being traded locally.

Geography

Garissa is located in eastern Kenya .The town lies along River Tana which is kenya's longest river
.The river provides water for agriculture.

Demographics

Majority of residents in the county are ethnic somalis especially the Ogaden clan
.90% of the population in the county are Muslims.

Tragic events

On April2,2015,Garissa University College was attacked by gunmen from the Al-shabaab group resulting in the deaths of 147 people and atleast 79 people injured
